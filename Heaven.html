1. Best place to be reborn in

Even the higher sensual realms are so pleasant that no one bothers to meditate or make spiritual progress. (“Which Sutta Says That Human Realm Is the Best Realm to Practice the Dhamma? - Q & A” 2016) A monk who upholds the five precepts is raised up to heaven, seemingly as if he has no other choice. (“Bhikkhusutta—Bhikkhu Sujato” n.d.) A human birth is desirable for developing bodhicitta. (“Which Sutta Says That Human Realm Is the Best Realm to Practice the Dhamma? - Q & A” 2016)

2. Recalling the Dhamma as a Deva

When the teachings have been well remembered and understood, when reborn as a Deva, he can expect to slowly but surely recall the teachings in various ways. (“AN 4:191 Sotānugata Sutta | Followed by Ear” n.d.)

6. Group Manifestation

If indeed the world is created by the karma its inhabitants, then it should be possible to create a realm of your design by collaborating the desires of many individuals onto a shared objective. Have one design, and apply unto it the desire of many, with the karma to match.
