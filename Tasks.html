Here lies a loosely connected collection of tasks which may be pertinent to the goal.

1. TODO Establish the proper tendencies

So that even in dreams, and at the time of death, the mind inclines towards the goal, as well as inferior alternatives as backups.

2. TODO Gain the conditions for relief

Grow and retain the conditions necessary for the attainment of ultimate relief, and inferior relief as backup.

3. TODO Master lucid dreaming

As this is the best gateway I have until master of samAdhi and siddhis.

4. TODO Accumulate puNya

By giving the gift of relief and its path to as many as possible, especially to those deserved currently on the path. The more wealth and power you are able to generate, the more puNya you can gather.

5. TODO Collect testimonies

Of induviduals who claim knowledge of the workings of karma as it pertains to rebirth in the heavenly realms, as well as it pertains to liberation.

6. TODO Develop Samadhi, and its foundations

To maximize certainty, take up the path, train the body, and set up the right conditions so that Samadhi can be made as certain as possible.

7. TODO Develop siddhis

To percieve the truths directly.

8. TODO Observe ALL the precepts

As many as possible from all traditions, but especially Buddhist and Dharmic. Observe Uposatha.

9. Truths to be Uncovered or Verified

9.1. TODO Stability and Universality of the Theravada Mechanics

Will this provisional axiom ever cease to hold true in the future? Are there any beings who do not experience stress in this way?

9.2. TODO dukkha? If so, samudaya? If so, nirodha? And if so, magga?

Uncover the veracity of the Four Noble Truths.

9.2.1. TODO If so, what is the true magga?

Is the true path still existant in this world? If so, where? Does the current Pali Canon suffice?

9.3. TODO Authenticity of the Pali Canon

Including minor texts like the Vimanavatthu. Have they been modified, and who were the original authors? Were they expressing genuine experiences or were they fabricated? If so, which parts were fabricated?

10. TODO Syncretic Living Hypothesis

There are many claims of causality with desirable outcomes. Without knowledge of their validity, if one could adhere to most or all of these claims, then he could maximize his chances of attaining those favorable outcomes if those claims indeed turn out to be true.

11. TODO Epistemic Validation of the Network of Claims

Especially pertaining to those who claim to see, reach epistemic certainty of those claims through application of epistemic methods of analysis. Corroboration is one of them.

12. TODO Epistemic Computer

An expert system where structured knowledge is used to derive conclusions through various epistemic methods.

13. TODO Public Thinktank

Allow collaboration of ideas with respect to the goals of this organisation.

14. TODO Envision Ideal States of Existence

Illustrate and bring to life with detail ideal states of existence, for example life as a deva, so that they may be imprinted into the mind in the case that rebirth by choice is possible.

15. TODO Availability of this knowledge beyond death

Explore ways the that the knowledge generated by this project could be accessed by induviduals after the death of this body.

16. TODO Causality

Does one apprehension of consciousness with or without judgement influence the variables of future instances?

17. TODO Search for a new paradigm of existence

This world is unsuitible for an ideal of control, as phenomena does not easily conform to desire.

18. TODO Digitize and translate all the sanskrit manuscripts

19. TODO Apotheosis Art

Deified versions of people for illustration of contrast.

20. TODO The Empyrean Codex

21. TODO Automatic Expert System

Using AI and knowledge representation technologies, scrape all relevant data and create an expert system which can be queried for perfectly relevant and accurate responses, and be used as an AI consultant for other AI agents.

22. TODO Research Devas according to the suttas

23. TODO Create alternate realities

If indeed the quality of rebirth is influenced by the mind, then having a clear image of the desired reality will be highly beneficial. Recreate it to be experienced digitally, perhaps in VR.

23.1. Refined Heaven

Essentailly a refined reflection of our lives here on earth.

23.1.1. Flexibility, Intensity, Efficiency

Whatever one desires, he gets it swiftly. He does not get much of anything which he would be dissatisfied with. It is smoooth. Fast, slow, subtle, intense; however one wants it he gets it that way.

Typically you can imagine it as fast, digital, hyperspeed, virtual, ultra HD, 4k 120fps, HDR, brilliant, luminous, energetic, high, ecstatic, etc.

24. TODO Stability

Are the mechanics of stress stable across time? Will they ever change?
